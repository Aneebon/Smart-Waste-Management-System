Why Smart Waste Management?

Smart waste management is an innovative approach to handling waste efficiently. It reduces pollution, optimizes waste collection routes, and minimizes overflow in bins.

Consequences of Poor Waste Management

Without proper waste management, cities face problems like pollution, health hazards, and inefficient waste disposal. Overflowing bins can cause sanitation issues and attract pests.

How Our System Helps

  • Automated alerts for bin collection
  • Optimized waste disposal routes
  • Better city cleanliness and hygiene
